Switching Load By Touch

The touch plate is connected to a 555 timer IC trigger pin. The 555 timer IC delivers a high output for a fixed duration of time as decided by its RC timing constant that is connected to the timer. This now drives the relay that in turn switches on the load for a particular duration of time and later switches it off.
